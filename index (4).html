Our story

A café that feels like home.

The Noshery has been serving Isleworth for over 20 years. Built on one simple belief: food should be real, generous, and worth every penny.

Whether you're a builder grabbing an early start or a family having Sunday lunch — there's always a table for you here.

We cook everything fresh, keep our prices honest, and always send you off with a smile.
